Instructions


Please read the instructions carefully before starting the experiment!

On each trial, you will be given an action and asked to make ratings about how likely it was caused by a particular mental state. For example, you might be asked: Given a person is engaging in the action of "frowning", how likely was it caused by the mental state of "sadness"?

You will respond by moving a slider bar to indicate the likelihood of each trial from 0 (not at all likely) to 100 (extremely likely). You will then click "Next" to move to the next question.

Do not hit the reload or back buttons during the experiment unless you wish to exit the study.
